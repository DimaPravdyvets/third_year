Table S1: Information about samples ancient samples sequenced in this study including provenance, age (radiocarbon or stratigraphic information), and sequencing statistics (endogeneous content etc.). N_SNP_call corresponds to the number of sites from our ~6M sites that were call in each sample. Analysed Nuclear and mtDNA Analysed columns corresponds to sample that were (1) and were not (0) analysed for nuclear and mtDNA analyses respectively. mtDNA capture column indicates whether mtDNA was enriched using target capture (0, no; 1, yes).
